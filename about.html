About OpenClaw Index

OpenClaw Index tracks the market performance of public tokens in the OpenClaw and Moltbook ecosystem. All market data is sourced live from CoinGecko and refreshes every 60 seconds.

The index covers tokens listed under the Moltbook and OpenClaw-themed category on CoinGecko, ranked by market capitalization. Each token includes live pricing, 24-hour and 7-day performance, market cap, and trading volume.

What We Track

  • Public tokens in the OpenClaw and Moltbook ecosystem
  • Live prices and market caps via CoinGecko API
  • 24-hour and 7-day price performance
  • Trading volume and fully diluted valuations
  • Top gainers and biggest losers
